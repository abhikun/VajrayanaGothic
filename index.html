VAJRAYANA GOTHIC

A multimedia web novel about driving through the Eastern Himalayas while your understanding of relationships, the self, and enlightenment unravels in real time. A gonzo travelogue meets Buddhist metaphysics meets postcolonial anxiety meets extremely online psychoanalysis.

Fair warning: This narrator pathologizes everything, including his own pathologizing. He's unreliable in the most reliable way possible. The prose oscillates between ecstatic landscape description and merciless self-excavation. There are no clean resolutions, only increasingly ornate spirals of irony.

Welcome to Monyul. Welcome to the Bardo.
